About Kalakrit

Kalakrit stands as the premier cultural hub at ABES Engineering College, renowned for its dynamic events centered on dance, singing, and fashion that bring students together in celebration.

Beyond academics, the club empowers members to develop skills in leadership, teamwork, and self-expression, complementing their engineering studies with holistic growth opportunities.

With a track record of inter-college successes, Kalakrit organizes workshops, competitions, and community activities to nurture artistic talents and strengthen bonds among participants.

Highlighted Event

Manthan ’23 – Flagship Cultural Fest

Manthan ’23 serves as Kalakrit's signature event, offering newcomers a platform to display their skills in singing, dancing, fashion, and other creative domains.

This fest generates buzz among performers and spectators alike, honoring artistic endeavors while fostering a sense of togetherness and shared enthusiasm.

Past & Upcoming Events

Ground Zero

An introductory event for fresh talents, featuring solo and group performances in dance and music to kickstart the cultural year.

Roadies

A high-energy competition inspired by adventure shows, combining physical challenges with cultural showcases to test endurance and creativity.

Manthan

The annual flagship fest where students compete in multiple categories, celebrating diversity and artistic expression across the college.

Projects

Diwali Delight – Humans of ABES Initiative

As part of the Diwali celebrations, Kalakrit's team visited local communities and shared meals and treats with children in need through the Humans of ABES program.

The initiative aimed to bring the warmth of Diwali to those facing hardships, embodying the festival's essence of light and generosity.

How to Join Kalakrit

Joining Kalakrit is open to passionate students eager to engage in and lead cultural activities. We run annual recruitment drives to welcome new members.

  • Submit an application form during the recruitment period.
  • Shortlisted candidates are invited for a personal interview to discuss their interests.
  • The club includes specialized groups like Fashion, Music, and Dance, each with tailored recruitment processes.
